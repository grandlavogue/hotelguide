ACCOMMODATION REGULATION

Article 1 Application of provisions
1. The accommodation terms and conditions and related contracts entered into by this hotel shall be as stipulated in these terms and conditions, and matters not stipulated in these terms and conditions shall be governed by laws and customs.
2. Reservations made by credit card shall be subject to the provisions of these terms and conditions, and the details thereof shall be determined between the hotel and the credit card company.
3. Notwithstanding the provisions of the preceding paragraph, the Hotel may conclude necessary contracts to the extent that it does not violate the spirit of these regulations, laws and customs.

Article 2 Refusal of accommodation request
Our hotel may refuse your application for accommodation in the following cases:
1. When the application for accommodation is not based on these terms and conditions.
2. When the hotel is fully booked and there are no rooms available.
3. When there is a risk that the person seeking accommodation will use the accommodation facility in a manner that violates the provisions of laws and regulations or the maintenance of public order or good morals.
4. When it is clearly recognized that the person seeking accommodation is suffering from an infectious disease.
5. When requesting a special burden from the hotel regarding accommodation.
6. When a person seeking accommodation is found to be in possession of illegal drugs or pets.
7. When the hotel is unable to provide accommodation due to natural disasters, damage to facilities, or other unavoidable reasons.
8. When it violates the rules stipulated by the laws and regulations of the Kingdom of Cambodia.
9. A person seeking accommodation may engage in assault, intimidation, extortion, unreasonable demands, gambling, possession or use of prohibited substances, acts that cause significant inconvenience to other guests, or other acts related to accommodation or within the Hotel. When there is a risk of committing an act that violates the law or public order and morals.

Article 3 Clarification of name, etc.
If the hotel accepts an application for accommodation by the day before the accommodation date, the hotel may request the person seeking accommodation to clarify the following matters within a specified period:
1. Name, gender, nationality and occupation of the person seeking accommodation;
2. Other matters deemed necessary by the hotel

Article 4 Reservation deposit
1. Our hotel will guarantee your accommodation reservation application with the payment of the reservation deposit. We will also guarantee your reservation if you pay for your stay in advance by credit card.
2. If the provisions of Article 5 apply, the reservation deposit mentioned in the previous paragraph will be applied to the cancellation fee, and the remaining amount will be refunded to the reservation person. However, if you are making a reservation by credit card, please refer to Article 5.
3. If the hotel cancels a reservation, the hotel will notify the reservation person of the reservation number, reservation date, name of the person in charge of reservation, and cancellation fee.

Article 5 Cancellation of reservation by guest
For customers who complete the payment of the reservation deposit, the following cancellation fee will be charged according to the total amount of the canceled room.
1. More than 2 days before arrival date: Free
2. The day before arrival: 50% of the first day's accommodation fee
3. On the day of arrival: 100% of the first day's accommodation fee
4. No-show: Full amount of the booked room
5. If you wish to cancel a reservation made with a credit card through a travel agent, please contact your travel agent.

Article 6 Cancellation of reservation by hotel
1. Unless otherwise specified, the hotel may cancel the accommodation reservation in the following cases.
a. When a request pursuant to the provisions of Article 2, Paragraphs 1 to 9 has been made, but the request has not been responded to within the prescribed period.
2. If payment of the reservation deposit as set forth in Article 4, Paragraph 1 is requested, but the payment is not made within the prescribed period. If the hotel cancels the reservation pursuant to each of the preceding paragraphs, the hotel will refund the reservation application deposit.

Article 7 Registration
Guests must register the following information at the front desk of the hotel on the day of arrival.
1. Matters stipulated in Article 3, Paragraph 1.
2. Foreign guests: Passport number, date of entry into Cambodia.
3. Cambodian guests: National registration number.
4. Current address, contact information, occupation, date of birth.
5. Date and time of departure.
6. Other matters deemed necessary by the hotel.

Please note that the personal information you provide will be used only when necessary to achieve the purpose of your stay, and will not be used for any other purpose.

Article 8 Room usage time
1. Guests may use the hotel's guest rooms from 3:00 p.m. to 12:00 p.m. the next day. However, if you stay for consecutive nights, you can use it all day except for the day of arrival and the day of departure.

2. Notwithstanding the provisions of the preceding paragraph, if the guest room is used beyond the check-out time, the hotel may charge the following additional charges.：
a. Until 3pm: 30% of accommodation fee
b. Until 6pm: 50% of the accommodation fee
c. After 6pm: 100% of accommodation fee

3. Use before check-in time
a. Before 8:59am: 100% of accommodation fee
b. From 9:00 a.m. to 11:59 a.m. 50% of the accommodation fee
c. From 12:00 pm to 2:59 pm: 30% of the accommodation fee

SAFETY INFORMATION
[How to respond in an emergency]

In case of emergency, read the following guidelinesfor your safety:

In Case of Emergency
In case of emergency, please contact the front desk. Please check the location of exits, stairs, and firefighting equipment on each floor using the evacuation map located inside the guest room door.

A. Fire response
1. In case of fire/fire alarm sounds
➵ Please contact the front desk.
➵ Please keep your room key with you at all times.
➵ Stay calm and avoid panic.
➵ Head to the nearest emergency exit and evacuate.
➵ Please check the evacuation route map posted inside the guest room door for the location of the emergency exit.
➵ Before opening the cabin door, check with the back of your hand whether the doorknob is hot.
2. If the door is hot
➵ Stay in your room, close the door, and turn off the air conditioner. Place a wet towel under the door.
➵ Place it on a vent with smoke. Contact the front desk and let the staff know your whereabouts. ➵ Please wait for the in-house broadcast. Also, if there is smoke in the room, ➵ Stay as low as possible and remain calm.
➵ If you stay in your room, hang a sheet outside the window to show that you are trapped inside.
➵ Fill the bathtub with water, wet the sheets, and place them at the base of the door .
➵ Please pack it. Close the vent where black smoke is coming out.
➵ Open windows only if you can get some fresh air.
➵ If there is smoke or fire when you open the door, close it immediately and follow the instructions above when staying in the room.
➵ Please take action.

3. During Evacuation
➵ Please use the stairs instead of the elevator.
➵ If there is smoke, stay low and cover your mouth and nose with a wet cloth or towel. Please cover.
➵ If visibility is poor, you can evacuate without getting lost by walking along the wall while touching it with your hands.
➵ If you cannot find other guests or staff, please call for help.

4. Evacuate first
➵ Evacuate to the designated evacuation area and follow the instructions of hotel staff.
➵ Guests are encouraged to stay with other guests and staff at the evacuation site.
➵ Do not return to the building until instructed to re-enter.


B. General Emergency Response
1. Medical Emergency
➵ In case of sudden illness or injury, please contact the front desk immediately.
➵ While waiting for the arrival of an ambulance or medical staff arranged by the front desk.
➵ Please provide first aid to the extent possible.

2. Discovery of Suspicious Persons and Objects
➵ If you see any suspicious persons or objects, please contact the front desk immediately.
➵ Please do not touch the suspicious object and do not alert others.
➵ The front desk will review the situation and take necessary measures.

By following these response methods, you can safely evacuate in an emergency and minimize trouble. Hotel staff will also prioritize the safety of our guests, so please follow their instructions.
